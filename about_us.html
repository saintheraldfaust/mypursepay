Our Mission

We believe consumer technology should be humanely intelligent, aesthetic, and—we cannot stress this enough—aesthetic.

A critical step towards creating anything that is as useful as it is intended, is understanding what it is, and communicating it to the right facilitator. Ideas managed by the wrong agent is as destructive as miscommunication.
