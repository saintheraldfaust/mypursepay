Privacy Policy

Welcome to My Purse Pay! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app and services. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register for an account or use our services. We do not collect device information or users' financial information. We may collect usage data to improve our services, but this data is anonymized and does not identify individual users.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process transactions, and communicate with you. We may also use your data for analytics, security, and legal compliance. My Purse Pay is a product of My Purse Ltd, a registered legal entity. All personal data is processed in compliance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Device Contact Permission

To enhance your experience, My Purse Pay may request access to your device's contact list. This permission is used solely to autofill the recipient's phone number when you use VTU (Virtual Top-Up) services. We do not store, share, or use your contact information for any other purpose.

4. Storage Access Permission

My Purse Pay may request access to your device's storage. This permission is used to save transaction receipts locally on your device for your convenience. We do not access, store, or share any other files or data from your device's storage.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. All information you provide is encrypted and stored securely. We do not store your data longer than necessary for the functionality of the app.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to provide our services. We may share anonymized data for analytics and research purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also request a copy of your data or object to its processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@mypursepay.com.ng.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze app usage. You can manage your cookie preferences through your device settings.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before using their services.

10. Children's Privacy

My Purse Pay is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Nigeria. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website or through other communication channels.

13. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mypursepay.com.ng.
